Customer Review's

Butter chicken at Restaurant Perfecto is a delightful culinary experience. The chicken is tender, cooked to perfection, and immersed in a rich, creamy tomato sauce that boasts a harmonious blend of spices. The balance of sweetness and tanginess is spot on, making each bite incredibly satisfying. Accompanied by fluffy naan or aromatic basmati rice, the dish truly shines. The presentation is elegant, and the portion size is generous, ensuring a fulfilling meal. Overall, Restaurant Perfecto's butter chicken is a must-try for lovers of authentic Indian cuisine.

Perfecto's white sauce pasta is a divine delight! Creamy, velvety sauce coats each al dente pasta strand, creating a symphony of flavors. The sauce strikes the perfect balance between richness and subtlety, with hints of garlic and Parmesan tantalizing the taste buds. Each bite is a heavenly experience, leaving you craving for more. The portion size is generous, ensuring satisfaction with every serving. Combined with impeccable service and a cozy ambiance, Perfecto's white sauce pasta is a must-try for any pasta enthusiast.

Perfecto's Kurkure Momos offer a tantalizing twist to the traditional dumplings. Crispy on the outside, tender on the inside, each bite bursts with flavorful fillings. The fusion of the crunchy Kurkure coating with the succulent momo filling creates an addictive texture and taste sensation. Perfecto's rendition of this popular street food classic is a delightful marriage of innovation and tradition. Whether you're a momo enthusiast or craving something new, Perfecto's Kurkure Momos are a must-try culinary experience that will leave your taste buds craving for more.
